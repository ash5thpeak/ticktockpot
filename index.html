About Tick Tock Pot

Tick Tock Pot is a calculator which helps you to add your times together, be it hours or minutes.

Along the way you'll get a little readout of your entries and have the ability to save them on your device.

Tick Tock Pot asks if you can please give a small donation to John Taylor Hospice who provide specialist care for people living with a terminal illness and their families.
